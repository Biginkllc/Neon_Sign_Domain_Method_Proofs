## What NSDM Is

The Neon Sign Domain Method (NSDM) is a way to visualize domain names as *real-world neon or LED signage* before sale or licensing.

Instead of only listing domains as plain text, NSDM presents them as realistic storefront, window, or building signs. These visual prototypes help buyers ‚Äî such as pawnshops, jewelry stores, sign makers, auction houses, galleries, and Web3 brands ‚Äî picture how a domain will look in the physical world.

## Example Vertical Packs (Initial Focus)

1. **Precious Metals & Pawnshops**  
   Domains such as `14kwith.crypto` and `24kwith.crypto` visualized as pawnshop window neon.

2. **Crypto-Branded Retail Signage**  
   Domains using endings like `.bitcoin`, `.ltc`, `.bch` for shops, ATMs, and service providers.

3. **Auction Houses & Galleries**  
   Domains designed as exterior building signage or event branding for high-end auctions.

Additional verticals (bars, clubs, cannabis, photography galleries, etc.) may be added over time.
